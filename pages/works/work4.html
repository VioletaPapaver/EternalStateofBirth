Poesía de lo no-dicho

2019
Instalación: 2 libros de artista y 32 piezas de cera

Silence and poetry merge in “Poetry of the non-said” in order to find a way of expression and meaning through the non-said. The two artist books complement each other: the first one is composed of the cartographies belonging to the sound landscapes obtained through the 3D spectrograms of 32 poems on silence, which have been recorded; the second one shows the spaces open on the paper by the extraction of these 32 poems over a fragmented portrait, being just the absence of the poetry what remains, and enabling this way to see through the book, connecting different pages. The written poems are encapsulated in the wax pieces that extend to both sides. These pieces take their shape from the ear, making the empty space into material.

Huella de silencio

2019
Cast brass

Materialization of the inner void of the mouth when pronouncing each of the letters of the word "silecio" (silence)

Lied ohne Worte (Caja de Silencio)

2019
Wood, cast brass, music box mechanism

The cilinder of a music box has been substitued for a new one that shows the word "silencio". This way, the word silence breaks its semantics in order to obtain a new meaning, a new beeing that belongs to sound beyond words, discovering the sound of silence.
