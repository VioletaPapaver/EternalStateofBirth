POETIZER

2023
App developed with Flutter

“Poetizer” is an interactive poetry app developed with flutter for mobile devices. It proposes a series of actions to the user by using the device's sensors to set the mechanism in motion:

- shake the world to change it: by shaking the device, several images of the set change in a random way, as well as triggering the reproduction of one of their sounds, also randomly.

- hide to see: the light sensor controls the opacity of the images so that the darker the images are, the more the words hidden behind them can be seen, whose combination, as they are associated with the images, also changes with the shake.

The work proposes a poetic action composed of images, sounds and words that seeks to subvert established meanings and liberate the elements that compose it towards new ways of interpretation through randomness and free association.
